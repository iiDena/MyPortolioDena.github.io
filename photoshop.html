Photoshop Own Picture

Project Assigment

As part of Flow 1, you will be working with Photoshop. You must select at least one photo from the series of photos taken during the excursion. The photo (s) does not have to be one of the ones you took yourself. But no pictures from the web - the starting point must be "our own / mul student" material. The image (s) must be processed in Photoshop so that at least the following has been done: Remove an item: Something in the image must be removed and the background (the hole behind) must be filled in to match the image. Add an item: Something that was not originally in the picture needs to be inserted so that it looks like it was there from the start. Adjust the lighting conditions of the image or add a mood or color tone to the image

Details

  • Topic: Photo Editing
  • Softwares/tools: Photoshop
  • Project date: September - 2021

Result

Here is the orginal photo taken in Copenhagen vs the edited photo. I wanted to give the photo some more contrast and sharpen the colors more to make the photo look more alive on with some beautiful colors in the sky. I also removed some of the bikes and people in the back of the picture to give it a more clean look.
