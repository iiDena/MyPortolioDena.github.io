Fitness Commercial

Project Description

Produce a commercial video with some kind of purpose to catch peoples attention. Chose your audience and brand. We picked the brand "Sats" which is a fitness here in Denmark and we wanted our audience to be teenagers. we often see younger people getting more lazy these days with all their technology, this is why the purpose of our video commercial is to catch teens attention and decide to be more active instead of spending all day in front of a screen. Check out our video and read more about project details here.
