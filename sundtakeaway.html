SundTakeAway - Food Delivery Project

Project Description

This was my final exam, to create a product working together with a company to solve a problem for their company. I chose SundTakeAway because I noticed that they didn't have an app. Therefore I started my research about the industry and their company to figure out how to create the best possible solution. Using a bunch of ux tools and design skills I ended up with a very detailed figma interactive Prototype for their app and coded a homepage using html and css. The process is linked in this site !

Details

  • Topic: App, web, Company collab
  • Softwares/tools: Illustrator, Figma, Visual studio, HTML, CSS, Figjam
  • Project date: June - 2023
